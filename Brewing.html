Brewing Guides

Learn how to brew Beans coffee at home

V60 Pour Over

Medium-fine grind, 15g coffee to 250ml water, 2–3 minutes.

Recommended: Beans House Blend

French Press

Coarse grind, 20g coffee to 300ml water, steep for 4 minutes.

Recommended: Chocolatey Brazilian

AeroPress

Fine grind, inverted method, 14g coffee to 220ml water.

Recommended: Light Roast Ethiopian

Coffee Grinder

Match grind size to method: fine (espresso), medium (V60), coarse (French press). Grind fresh for best flavour.

Recommended: Beans House Blend

Cold Brew

Coarse grind, steep 12–18 hours in the fridge. Start ratio: 1:8 coffee to water, then dilute to taste.

Recommended: Chocolatey Brazilian

Moka Pot

Fill base to valve, medium-fine grind (not espresso-fine), brew on low heat. Remove when it starts to sputter.

Recommended: Light Roast Ethiopian
All recipes are starting points – adjust to taste. Visit our events page for hands-on brewing workshops.
